Buttons

To style buttons add .btn-library class. For the bigger button add class btn-lg and the smaller version add btn-sm.

Wrap group of buttons in a div <class="btn_group" role="group" aria-label=""> It will align group of buttons to the center. If you want to position left or right just add data-direction="prev" to align left and data-direction="next" to align right (it will require the div with classes 'btn_group' to work).

Tab

Here is the reference on how to apply tabs
